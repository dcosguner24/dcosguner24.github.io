About Me

"Driven by curiosity, continuous learning, and a desire to build practical solutions that genuinely improve people's lives."

I am a motivated and detail-oriented Mechanical Engineering student at Michigan State University (GPA 3.235) with a strong passion for product development, manufacturing, thermodynamics, and emerging technologies. I thrive at the intersection of rigorous engineering and human-centred design.

As an R&D Cooling Intern at BSH Home Appliances, I support refrigeration product development through engineering analysis, competitive benchmarking, design evaluation, and manufacturing-focused problem solving. This experience has deepened my understanding of how every engineering decision shapes product performance, cost, reliability, and the end customer's experience.

Beyond engineering, I am passionate about entrepreneurship. As co-founder of GreenRoomeo, I built a student-focused housing platform from scratch to help university students navigate housing decisions with confidence. I believe the best solutions combine technical rigour with a genuine understanding of the people they serve.

I am also a competitive swimmer — earning 2-time CAAC 1st Place in the 100m Breaststroke and placing 3rd at the MHSAA Boys Division 2 State Championship. I currently swim with the MSU University Club Team and hold an American Red Cross Lifeguard Certification. These pursuits have shaped my discipline, leadership, and ability to perform under pressure.
